Our Core Rainwater Harvesting Solutions

Rooftop Catchment System

Our integrated gutter system directs clean rainwater directly into storage tanks.

This is the most common and effective method for residential properties.

Borewell & Pit Recharge

We use percolation pits and borewell systems to replenish local groundwater tables.

This technique helps mitigate urban flooding and sustains underground water sources.

Advanced Filtration & Storage

Multi-stage filtration ensures the collected water is suitable for gardening and non-potable use.

Robust, durable storage tanks are installed to save water through dry seasons.
